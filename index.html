Confess

Send anonymous or named confessions to our page — nice design, no token exposed.

Secure via server

How it works

  1. Read the instructions, then press **Start confession**.
  2. Enter the name of the person (your crush) and your message.
  3. Choose if you want to remain anonymous.
  4. Press **Send** — we'll forward the confession to our Telegram (hidden token).
